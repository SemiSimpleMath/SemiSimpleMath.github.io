Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-10

This Privacy Policy describes how this messaging service (“we,” “us”) collects and uses information when sending and receiving text messages. The service provides two-way conversational messages from an automated assistant (“Emi”) for coordination and personal communication (for example, scheduling and status updates). This service is not used for marketing.

How users opt in

End users consent to receive messages by replying YES to an invitation message, or by otherwise providing explicit permission to receive messages from Emi. We only send messages to numbers that have explicitly opted in. Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Information we collect

We may collect and store:

  • Phone numbers (sender and recipient)
  • Message content
  • Message metadata, such as timestamps and delivery status

How we use information

  • Provide the messaging service (send and receive messages)
  • Maintain service reliability and troubleshoot delivery issues
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized use
  • Maintain limited logs for auditing and debugging

Sharing and disclosure

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We share information only with providers needed to operate the service, such as messaging providers (for example, Twilio) and hosting/infrastructure providers.
  • These providers process information only to provide services on our behalf.

Data retention

We retain message logs only as long as needed for troubleshooting, security, and audit purposes, and delete them periodically.

Your choices and controls

  • Opt out: Reply STOP to stop receiving messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for help.
  • Deletion requests: You can request deletion of retained logs by contacting us. We will make reasonable efforts to honor requests, subject to operational and legal requirements.
