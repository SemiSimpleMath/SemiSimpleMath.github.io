Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-10

This Privacy Policy describes how this personal messaging service (“we,” “us”) collects and uses information when sending and receiving text messages. This service is operated by an individual and is used to communicate with a limited set of personal contacts. It is not used for marketing.

Information we collect

We may collect and store:

  • Phone numbers (sender and recipient)
  • Message content (including attachments, if any)
  • Message metadata, such as timestamps, delivery status, and message routing information

How we use information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide the messaging service (send and receive messages)
  • Maintain service reliability and troubleshoot delivery issues
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized use
  • Maintain internal logs for auditing and debugging

Sharing and disclosure

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We share information only with service providers needed to operate the service, such as messaging providers (for example, Twilio) and hosting/infrastructure providers.
  • These providers process information only to provide services on our behalf.

Data retention

We retain message logs only as long as needed for troubleshooting, security, and audit purposes. Logs are deleted periodically.

Your choices and controls

  • Opt out: You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP.
  • Help: You can request help by replying HELP.
  • Deletion requests: You can request deletion of retained logs by contacting us. We will make reasonable efforts to honor requests, subject to operational and legal requirements.
