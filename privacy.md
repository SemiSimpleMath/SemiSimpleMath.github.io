# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-10

This is a personal messaging line used by one person to communicate with a small set of friends and family.

## What data we collect
- Phone numbers (sender and recipient)
- Message content
- Message timestamps and delivery status

## How we use data
- To send and receive messages
- To troubleshoot delivery issues and prevent abuse

## Sharing
- We do not sell personal information.
- Data is shared only with providers needed to deliver messages (for example, Twilio) and hosting providers, solely to operate this service.

## Retention
- Message logs are kept for troubleshooting and audit purposes, then deleted periodically.

## Your choices
- You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.
- You can request deletion by contacting us.
